Effective Date: December 2025

We believe in data minimization. The Trivia Bot collects only the minimum amount of data required to function:

  • Discord User ID: To track scores, streaks, and rankings.
  • Username & Avatar: Displayed temporarily on Rank Cards (not permanently stored).

Your data is used strictly for gameplay mechanics:
