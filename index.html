Core Protocol

The trivia loop runs automatically in designated channels.

/start [channel]
  • Questions spawn every 20 minutes.
  • Categories: History, Science, Anime, Vocaloid (AI).
  • Correct answers build your Streak.
  • Wrong answers break streaks unless you own a Backup Drive.

Duel Systems

Challenge other users to 1v1 trivia battles. 5 Rounds.

/duel [user] [wager]
  • Normal Mode: Friendly practice. No fees.
  • Risky Mode: 50 CR Fee. 20h Cooldown.
  • Theft: Winning 5-0 steals 30% of loser's wallet.
  • Spectators can Bet on the outcome.

Boss Raids

Cross-server events where a massive Boss spawns.

/raid_start [name]
  • Questions drop rapidly (every 20s).
  • Correct Answer: 500 Damage.
  • Wrong Answer: Boss Heals 50 HP.
  • Loot: Credits based on damage + Random Rare Drops.
